# SOUNDS OF LESBOS · SOL

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **I THINK I'M IN LESBIANS WITH YOU**.

SOUNDS OF LESBOS (SOL) — no recording of antiquity survives and Sappho's melodies are lost, but the pitch-data did not die: this card synthesises them LIVE on a plucked-string (Karplus–Strong) voice from real ancient material — the octave species (Sappho's Mixolydian, the Dorian), Pythagorean tuning (tone 9:8, leimma 256:243), the true quarter-tones of the enharmonic genus, and the actual quantitative Sapphic metre as beats. A seven-string lyre you can pluck. Two-layer honest: the pitches, modes and metre are real antiquity; the timbre is a modern synthesis (no recordings exist), and the Seikilos-mode phrase is an evocation, not the surviving score. Pairs with [[sappho]] and feeds [[limen-sapphic-carrier]].
